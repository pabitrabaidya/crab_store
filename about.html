Who We Are

Baidya Crab House is a trusted local seafood business specializing in fresh and live river crabs sourced directly from the Sundarbans region of West Bengal. We work closely with experienced local fishermen to ensure that every crab we deliver meets high standards of freshness, quality, and hygiene.

Our focus is to remove unnecessary middlemen and bring premium-quality crabs straight from the river to your kitchen. We proudly serve customers across Kolkata and nearby areas with reliable service and honest pricing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make fresh, healthy, and authentic seafood easily accessible to everyone. We aim to build long-term trust with our customers by maintaining transparency, fair pricing, and consistent quality in every order we deliver.
